Hi, I'm Nien. Pursuer of great travel experiences.

I travel to destinations with a story to tell, seeking local specialities, off-the-beaten-track experiences and jaw-dropping natural scenery. I am drawn to the culture and daily life that has shaped the lives of everyone here.

At Trip Anywhere I talk about my travels in local tourist destinations.

In doing so, I hope to promote a richer understanding of the culture and life of the people where I come in and build more developed tourism.
